Gretcheniki

They are prepared using ground chicken, ground beef, turkey, and pork.

Cook It Now

Instructions

  • Step 1Rinse the buckwheat thoroughly and cook like regular porridge, but without salt or other spices. Butter or vegetable oil is also not needed at this stage.
  • Step 2While the buckwheat is cooling, remove the minced meat from the refrigerator and place in a large convenient bowl.
  • Step 3Add 2 eggs and finely grated onion to the meat ingredient.
  • Step 4Put buckwheat porridge to the main mass and mix everything well. If desired, you can add chopped dill, parsley or cilantro.
  • Step 5Put a frying pan on the fire and add vegetable oil. While the pan is heating, form small patties with your hands and roll them in flour.
  • Step 6Fry the buckwheat until golden brown on each side. Once cooked, place the patties on a paper towel to remove excess fat.

Cooking Time

1 h 30 min

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef600 g
  • Buckwheat (Groups)150 g
  • Eggs2 pcs
  • Onion1 pc
  • Vegetable OilFor Frying
  • GreensTo Taste
